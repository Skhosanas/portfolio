WHO ARE WE?

Mvelase Mining and Logistics is a South African Black Owned company that specialises in mining and is invested in the processing of minerals locally. As Mvelase Mining and Logistics we endeavour to bridge that gap by working closely with Zimbabwe’s government and add value by establishing Lithium, Chrome,Anthracite Coal, Iron Ore and Copper processing plants.

VISION

At Mvelase Mining Group our vision is to be a leading and sustainable chrome ore ,lithium , coal , gold and copper producer, recognized for our commitment to safety, environmental stewardship, and social responsibility. We aim to create long-term value for our stakeholders, employees, and communities, while innovating and improving our operations to meet the evolving needs of our customers and the industry.
